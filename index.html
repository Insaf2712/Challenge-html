Brownies Recipe

Fudgy, sweet, and sprinkled with a pinch of joy ✨🍫

Preparation time:

  • Preparation time : 20 min
  • Cooking time : 20-25 min

Ingrediets:

  • 170g melted butter
  • 180g sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 100g melted chocolate
  • 110g flour
  • 30g cacoa powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • 100g chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C , and Prepare your baking pan with parchment paper.
  2. Melt the butter and chocolate together, then let the mixture cool slightly.
  3. In a separate bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until smooth. Add the vanilla and mix well.
  4. Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and stir until combined.
  5. Add the dry ingredients (flour, cacao powder, and salt), Mix gently until just combined (do not overmix).
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. You can add any topping you want (optional).
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  9. Bake for 20–25 minutes, until the edges are set and the center is slightly soft.
  10. Let cool completely before slicing and serving.
